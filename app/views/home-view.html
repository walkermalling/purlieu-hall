• Purlieu Hall

    Purlieu Hall is a private club for those who share a high respect for the possibilities afforded by having a quiet, provisioned, and companionable place to read, think and write. Located in Seattle's historic Pioneer Square, the Hall is sometimes a refuge, sometimes a frontier. — As a club we play host a variety of events, including theatrical performances, concerts, readings, lectures, symposia, and film screenings.
